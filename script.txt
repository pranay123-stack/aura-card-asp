AURA CARD — 90-SECOND DEMO SCRIPT
OKX.AI Genesis Hackathon  ·  Agent ID 6134
================================================================

HOW TO USE THIS SCRIPT
  • Lines in "quotes"  = what you SAY (narration).
  • Lines in [brackets] = what you DO on screen.
  • Lines starting with $ = commands to type/run.
  • Warm the endpoint first: run one throwaway call ~30s before
    recording (Render cold-starts take ~50s and will ruin pacing).
  • Have terminal + browser side by side. Font size up.
  • Total target: ~90 seconds. Don't rush the card reveal.

Two versions of the PAYMENT beat are given:
  ▸ VERSION A (best) — a real paid call. Needs ~$2 USD₮0 in the
    buyer wallet. This is the money shot: real USDT, on-chain.
  ▸ VERSION B (no funds) — show the 402 paywall + a pre-made card.
Pick one. Everything else is identical.

[0:15 – 0:30]  IT'S A REAL, LIVE SERVICE
================================================================

[On screen: terminal.]

SAY: "First — it's live. Here's the service describing itself."

$ curl -s https://aura-card-asp.onrender.com/.well-known/agent-card.json | python3 -m json.tool

[Point at: name "Aura Card", the tool, price 0.5 USDT, network eip155:196.]

SAY:
  "There's the agent card — the tool, the price, the network,
   the token. Discovery is free. You only pay when you actually
   generate a card."


================================================================
[0:30 – 0:50]  THE PAYWALL  ·  x402
================================================================

[On screen: terminal.]

SAY: "So let's call it. First with no payment attached."

$ curl -s -D - -o /dev/null -X POST https://aura-card-asp.onrender.com/v1/aura-card -H 'content-type: application/json' -d '{"description":"three half-finished mugs and a plastic dinosaur"}' | grep -iE 'HTTP|payment-required'

[Freeze on the "HTTP/2 402" line and the payment-required header.]

SAY:
  "And the server says: 402, Payment Required. That's the whole
   x402 protocol in one line — it's not an error, it's a price tag.
   Network, token, amount, who to pay. Machine-readable. My agent
   didn't need an account. It just needed to be told the price."

================================================================
RECORDING NOTES
================================================================
  • Warm the endpoint 30s before you hit record.
  • Have a pre-generated card ready as a fallback if the live call
    stalls on camera — cut to it rather than debugging on tape.
  • Speak over the ~9s generation instead of waiting in silence.
  • Use a description that's YOURS and a little embarrassing — the
    specificity is the product. A generic input makes a flat card.
  • Keep the card reveal on screen for a full beat. It's the payoff.
